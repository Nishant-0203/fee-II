SHAH RUKH KHAN

Shah Rukh Khan

Article

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as SRK, is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality who works in Hindi films. Referred to in the media as the "Baadshah of Bollywood", "King Khan", and "King of Romance", he has appeared in more than 80 films and earned numerous accolades, including 14 Filmfare Awards. Khan has a significant following in Asia and the Indian diaspora worldwide. In terms of audience size and income, he has been described as one of the most successful film stars in the world.

History

Shah Rukh Khan was born on November 2, 1965, in New Delhi, India. He made his Bollywood debut in the film "Deewana" in 1992. Before entering the film industry, he worked in television series such as "Fauji" and "Circus". His films from the 1990s like "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge", "Dil To Pagal Hai", and "Kuch Kuch Hota Hai" were massive hits and cemented his status as a leading actor in Bollywood.

Over the years, Khan has starred in many critically acclaimed films and box office hits including "Devdas", "Swades", "Chak De! India", "My Name Is Khan", and "Chennai Express". He is also the co-owner of the Kolkata Knight Riders, a team in the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Shah Rukh Khan's contribution to the film industry has been recognized with several awards and honors. He was awarded the Padma Shri by the Government of India in 2005 and has been honored by the governments of France and Malaysia.
